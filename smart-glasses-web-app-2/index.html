What is EYRA?

EYRA is an inclusive technology brand building the world's most accessible smart glasses. Designed to empower visually impaired individuals, our glasses describe scenes, read out texts, and provide intelligent real-world interactions using AI, ChatGPT, and OpenCV.

Our Journey

Started as a science project by passionate innovators, EYRA evolved into a mission to help people gain their independence. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the world with clarity and confidence.
